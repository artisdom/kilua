kilua v_VERSION

  Kilua was put together by Steve Kemp <https://steve.kemp.fi/>,
  based upon the prototype editor Antirez wrote and introduced
  upon his blog <http://antirez.com/news/108>.

  Major keybindings:

    Purpose             | Binding
    --------------------------------
    Exit                | Ctrl-q
    Create a new buffer | Ctrl-x c
    Switch buffers      | Ctrl-x b
    Read a file         | Ctrl-x o
    Save your file      | Ctrl-x s
    Evaluate lua        | M-x
